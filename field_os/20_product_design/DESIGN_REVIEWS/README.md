# DESIGN_REVIEWS

## What lives here

**Design reviews** — applications of `DESIGN_REVIEW_PROTOCOL.md` to the artifacts in this folder. Each review records the artifact examined, the checks run, any BLOCKER failures, and the outcome: **Accept**, **Revise**, or **Reject**. Reviews are the gate between workflow stages.

- **Purpose.** Verify each design artifact stays faithful to approved architecture and experience before it advances.
- **Inputs.** The artifact under review (journey, card, wireframe, or prototype); `DESIGN_REVIEW_PROTOCOL.md`; the immutable documents it must honor.
- **Outputs.** One review record per artifact with outcome and cited failed checks. Gates movement in `EXPERIENCE_BLUEPRINT.md`.
- **Dependencies.** `DESIGN_REVIEW_PROTOCOL.md`; the artifact and its architectural references.
- **Responsible role.** Design Reviewer.
- **Relationship with the approved architecture.** The review measures conformance to it. The protocol introduces no principle; where protocol and an immutable document appear to disagree, the immutable document governs and the review is corrected.
